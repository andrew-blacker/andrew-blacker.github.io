Recipe for Best Mac and Cheese

Ingredient Name Amount Picture of ingredient
Elbow Macaroni 8 Ounces
Butter 1/4 cup
All-Purpose Flour 1/4 Cup
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Milk 2 Cups
Shredded Cheddar Cheese 2.5 Cups

How to make the Mac and Cheese:

  1. Boil a pot of water and cook the elbow macaroni until al dente(8-10mins).
  2. Melt the butter and as it is melting, combine the butter, flour, and salt and stir until the sauce is smooth. Slowly add the milk and thoroughly stir the mixture
  3. Add the cups of shredded cheddar cheese into the mixture until the cheese has melted completed..
  4. Drain the water from the elbow macaroni and combine the noodle with the sauce. Stir until the noodles are well coated and this dish is ready to eat
